TAKING HUMANS TO SPACE

The universe contains countless planets and moons, each playing a key role in the cosmic structure. Planets vary in size and composition, from rocky worlds like Earth to gas giants like Jupiter. Many exist in habitable zones where life might be possible. Moons orbit planets and differ in nature, with some potentially supporting life. Beyond our Solar System, exoplanets and their moons are being discovered, revealing an incredible diversity of worlds in the vast universe. These celestial bodies help us understand planetary formation and the potential for life. Our Cosmic corner will take you on a tour to space to explore Our solar system planets and Satellites.
